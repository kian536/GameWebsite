Nightbright was established as a result of a common vision and individual experience. Being a university student myself, I personally have a passion for playing fan games made by others so I believe it's only right to support them financially.

I realised that finding some of these fantastic made games are quite difficult and can be frustrating. This insight gave rise to the concept of Nightbright, an online storefront created to make it simple for people of all ages but specifically gaming students to access a variety of fan made games.

At Nightbright, I believe everyone should contribute to giving little change to those who worked on the game projects so it benifits them in the long run and perhaps motivates them to keep going. I tried my best to provide fair rates on all of the advertised games.

Purchasing has never been simpler thanks to our streamlined ordering process and user-friendly.



We set out on a mission to develop a platform that would revolutionise the way students access their important materials out of our enthusiasm for education and our desire to empower other students.
